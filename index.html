NeuroAI
Initiative

A curated series of conversations and convenings engaging open questions at the intersection of neuroscience, neural interfaces, and artificial intelligence.

Drawing from New York City's unusually rich, cross-disciplinary ecosystem across neuroscience, neural interfaces, AI, medicine, research, and industry, the NeuroAI Initiative convenes conversations across neuro for AI and AI for neuro.

These gatherings are designed to build on one another over time, creating a live exchange in which questions are refined, contested, and gradually developed into a shared roadmap.
